Africa has a rich background with diverse cultures, musics and foods, amongst others that have been past on from generations is the African tales. They are traditional stories passed down by elders to children, imparting wisdom and guiding their way of life. It is usually told in a particular setting that involves children sitting in circles at night, and some would include drum to make it fun.This was traditionally called 'Tales by Moonlight'. However, this stories are taught using animals like tortoise, crocodiles, elephants, birds, etc.
